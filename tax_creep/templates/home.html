How it works

New Zealand has not adjusted its tax brackets for inflation since 2010. Therefore if inflation is, say, 6%, and you get a 6% payrise, your after tax income has not grown by 6%, but less!

This calculator takes your income, and the current inflation rate, and calculates how much payrise you actually need, in order to increase your after-tax income to match inflation.
